Rainbow Dessert Bowls

These edible dessert bowls are as delicious as they are adorable. Try the single-serve, ice cream-filled treats as an alternative to cupcakes for birthdays or class parties.!


Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons salted butter
  • 1 (10-oz.) bag miniature marshmallows
  • 1 (11-oz.) box fruit-flavored rice cereal (such as Fruity Pebbles)
  • Ice cream or sherbet of choice
  • Whipped Cream
  • Red maraschino cherries
  • Rainbow candy sprinkles

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large Dutch oven over medium-low. Add marshmallows, and cook, stirring constantly until smooth. Remove from heat, and stir in cereal until well coated.
  2. Lightly coat 16 muffin cups of 2 muffin pans with cooking spray. Divide cereal mixture among muffin cups, and press to form into the shape of the cup. Use the back of a spoon lightly coated in cooking spray, to form a “well” in the center of each cup. Chill 15 minutes.
  3. Fill each cup with 1/4 cup scoop of ice cream. Top with whipped cream, a cherry, and sprinkles.
