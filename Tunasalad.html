Tuna salad

Ingredients:

  • Two bottles of tuna preserved in water
  • 2 large red onions soaked for five minutes in cold water
  • 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped celery
  • 1 tablespoon coarse mustard
  • One third cup of mayonnaise
  • Ground black pepper
  • Lemon juice

Steps:

Break up the tuna in a small bowl with a fork. Add onions, parsley, celery, and mix the ingredients well. Add mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and black pepper, then add lemon juice
