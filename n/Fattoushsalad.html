Fattoush with yogurt salad

Ingredients:

  • chopped lettuce
  • Tow tomatoes, cut into medium pieces
  • Two grains of cucumber are cut into circles
  • Chopped onion
  • Green peppers, cut into cubes
  • Toast

Sauce ingredients:

  • Half a cup of yogurt
  • Half a teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon sumac

Steps:

Chop lettuce vegetables, with tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and green peppers in a large bowl Mix yogurt, salt, pepper, olive oil, lemon juice and sumac in a bowl. Pour the sauce over the salad, and stir well Distribute toast on the face and serve
